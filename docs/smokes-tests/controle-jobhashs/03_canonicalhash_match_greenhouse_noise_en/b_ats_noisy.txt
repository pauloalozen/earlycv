Job description
We are looking for a Senior Data Analyst to support business teams with analytics, dashboards, and KPI modeling.

What you will do
- Build dashboards in Power BI.
- Write SQL queries for data analysis.
- Define and monitor KPIs.
- Support business teams in data-driven decision making.

What we are looking for
- Advanced experience with SQL.
- Experience with Power BI.
- Knowledge of data modeling.
- Ability to communicate with business stakeholders.
