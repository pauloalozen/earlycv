Full job description
We are looking for an Analytics Engineer to build reliable analytics datasets and self-serve dashboards for go-to-market teams.

Responsibilities:
- Model data in dbt.
- Build and maintain dashboards in Looker.
- Partner with sales and marketing teams on KPI definitions.
- Improve data quality across analytics workflows.

Requirements:
- Strong SQL skills.
- Experience with dbt.
- Experience with BI tools such as Looker.
- Ability to work with business stakeholders.
